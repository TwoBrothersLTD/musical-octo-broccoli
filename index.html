About the Podcast

The Two Brothers Podcast is hosted by two 20-something-year-olds who mix banter, real talk, and good vibes. We sit down with athletes, entrepreneurs, and all-around inspiring people to get into their journey — raw, honest, and always entertaining.

Episode 1 — Andy Crittenden: From Black Belt to Business Boss

In our first ever episode, we sit down with Andy Crittenden — a martial artist, business owner, and all-around powerhouse. We talk discipline, mindset, and how to fight for what you want (literally and metaphorically).
